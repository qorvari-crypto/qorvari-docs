# Qorvari: Vision, Motto, & Strategic Plan

## Our Motto
*"Autonomous Discovery. Infinite Scale."*

## Corporate Vision
Qorvari envisions a world where enterprise infrastructure discovery, cloud migration, and optimization are entirely frictionless and fully automated. By removing the dependency on massive consultant teams and heavy legacy toolchains, Qorvari democratizes enterprise-grade cloud management. We aim to be the definitive **Hybrid Open-Core SaaS Platform** operated efficiently by a specialized Artificial Intelligence workforce.

## The Strategy (Vision Plan)
We are leveraging years of mature intellectual property (formerly Matilda Cloud) to leapfrog the market. 

### Core Product Directives
1.  **Product-Led Growth (The Open Core):** We launch the core discovery and assessment engine as a free, open-source Community tier. This acts as a massive inbound marketing funnel. 
2.  **Frictionless SaaS (The Pro Tier):** Mid-market clients who love the open-source tool will inevitably want it "managed." We seamlessly convert them to a ~$99/month multi-tenant SaaS offering with no setup required.
3.  **Enterprise Lock-In (The Premium Tier):** Large enterprises demand optimization and AIOps. We restrict infrastructure-as-code generation (GenIAC) and advanced analytics to high-ticket enterprise contracts.

### Operational Directives
1.  **Zero Human Bloat:** To maintain immense profitability, Qorvari operates via an **Autonomous Enterprise Model**. We replace 50+ human engineers, marketers, and testers with specialized AI Agents operating beneath the Solo Founder.
2.  **Industrial Standardization:** We abide by the highest strictures of enterprise architecture documentation (ADRs, OpenAPI, PRDs, SOC-2 compliant security scanning) so that an AI-generated codebase can pass stringent Fortune 500 technical audits.
